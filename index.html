Fabric as proof

The cloth decides how a garment falls.

A suit is only as good as the length it is cut from. Our fabric wall is built around Reid & Taylor suiting and a considered range of wool, cotton, silk, linen, and refined blends — chosen for drape, season, and how they hold a press.

Come in and handle the cloth. We will talk you through weight, weave, and which fabric suits the occasion and the Bengaluru climate.

How a commission works

Five honest steps, from first word to final press.

No mystery, no overpromising. This is exactly how your garment comes together.

Step 01

Consultation

We discuss the occasion, your wardrobe, and the look you want — measured advice, no pressure.

Step 02

Fabric selection

Choose from Reid & Taylor suiting and our fabric wall, guided by drape, season, and use.

Step 03

Measurement

Precise measurements taken to build your pattern and hold a clean line through the body.

Step 04

Fitting & trial

A trial fitting to check balance, posture, and comfort — adjusted until it sits right.

Step 05

Finishing & delivery

Careful pressing and finishing, then delivery — usually one to two weeks; express on request.
